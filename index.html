Below are the instruction on how to play the game:
  • Step 1: Click on 'START' button to start.

  • Step 2: When game start, the 3x3 dark-gray box will be highlighted in RED one by one random. MEMORIES the sequence until it stop highlighting.

  • Step 3: Click on the box on which you memories it. If all answer CORRECT, you will move to the next round. Otherwise, the game will be STOP and re-start the game back to round 1.


  • NOTE:
    The gray box will be highlighted randomly. How many time will be depends on the number of rounds. e.g if you in round 3, the box will be highlighted 3 times.
